Map Mania!

Hello, Welcome to Map Mania! This is a game where you will try and find ten of my favorite locations around the world! The less clicks it takes you to find the locations, the higher your score! Every time you click, a marker will be placed. If you found the location, you will be notified and the location will show up on the map for the rest of the game. I have a slider that will give you some hints if you are getting hotter or colder, remember, the closer you zoom in, the better your chances are of finding it! Best of luck and lets see how well you know me!
